True Vanilla is a fully vanilla semi-anarchy Minecraft server with an authentic survival experience. We aim to provide a vanilla, community-driven environment where players can enjoy the game as it was meant to be played - no /homes, no /tpa, no P2W, just pure survival.

Whether you prefer to adventure alone, fight deadly battles, or work together on massive builds, there’s something for everyone here.

We strive to maintain a fair environment, so please read and follow the rules in the Discord to ensure a great experience for everyone.
