Welcome to True Vanilla!

True Vanilla is a fully vanilla Minecraft server that offers an authentic survival experience. Our goal is to provide a fair, community-driven environment where players can enjoy the game as it was meant to be played—no mods, no pay-to-win, just pure survival.

Our community is built on trust and cooperation. Whether you prefer to adventure alone or work together on massive builds, there’s something for everyone here. We encourage creativity, exploration, and friendly competition.

What You Can Expect:

  • 100% vanilla gameplay with no modifications.
  • A dedicated community of like-minded players.
  • Seamless multi-world support, including the Nether and the End.
  • Events and occasional challenges to keep things exciting.
  • A Discord server where you can chat with other players and stay updated.


We strive to maintain a welcoming and fair environment, so make sure to read and follow the rules to ensure a great experience for everyone.
