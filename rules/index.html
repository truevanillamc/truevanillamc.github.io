Discord Rules


  • No racism, bigotry, hate-speech, etc.
    Trying to circumvent this rule will be treated the same.
  • Be respectful and civil
    Not doing so will lead to a ban
  • No TOS/NSFW/illegal content
    This includes conversations, links, images, and emotes.
    NO flashing or blinking images/emotes.
  • No Advertising
    This includes promotion of third parties, other guilds and minecraft servers, unless I have permitted you to do so

SMP Rules

  • Griefing (such as stealing) is allowed, except in the following cases:
    When done in large/mass quantities
    When done near spawn (2k blocks out from 0X 0Y)
    You may not kill another player's villager(s), even when griefing their base
  • Wither Spawning
    Withers must not be spawned or released above ground 1k blocks from spawn or on the main End island.
  • Duping
    Duping is not allowed, excluding TNT, rails, gravel & sand (NOT dragon egg), string (dm @[Blue Lightning] in discord to extend this)
  • Lag
    Any machine, project, or farm which is causing considerable lag may be asked to be removed.
  • In-game Chat
    The discord rules apply to the in-game chat, signs, blocks in a shape, or any form of communication in the server.
  • No Hacking
    No hacking is permitted in any way possible, unless conflicting with rule 7.
  • Combat Logging
    Combat logging is not allowed in terms of PvP, doing this while in lava etc. is fine otherwise.


We reserve the right to change these at any given moment without alerting the users, so please check these every once in a while.
